<h1>
eXo Platform 3.5<br />
<span class="TxtCloudReady">The Cloud-Ready Portal and User Experience Platform</span></h1>
<span class="TxtTitle">eXo Platform is the first and only integrated user experience platform for building and deploying transactional websites, managing web and social content and creating gadgets and dashboards. </span>
<ul class="ContenLeft">
<li>
<b>An Enterprise Portal Foundation:</b> a lightweight, standards-based portal for integrating applications, portlets and gadgets, with security features like access control and single sign-on.</li>
<li>
<b>Complete Enterprise Content Management:</b> easily build websites, author and publish content, capture and organize documents and unstructured content, and store everything in the built-in Java Content Repository.</li>
<li>
<b>Social Networks &amp; Collaboration:</b> transform your portal-based app into a vibrant online community by adding forum, chat, calendars, wikis and more; support for OpenSocial allows users to create and connect rich user profiles, publish activity streams, and collaborate in dedicated spaces.</li>
<li>
<b>Development Platform:</b> a full-featured development environment for building and integrating applications, gadgets and mashups</li>
<li>
<b>Cloud-Ready:</b> multi-tenancy at the JVM level optimizes resource utilization and reduces the cost of deploying intranets and websites within a customer's private cloud environment.</li>
<li>
<b>Mobile:</b> native iPhone, iPad, and Android apps let you take your intranet with you. Access your personalized gadget dashboard, activity stream, document repository, and more. <a class="ColorBlue" href="http://www.exoplatform.com/company/en/platform/mobile">Learn more about eXo Mobile Apps</a></li>
</ul>
